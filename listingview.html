About this Property

Experience the pinnacle of luxury living in this architectural masterpiece located in the exclusive Trousdale Estates of Beverly Hills. Designed with meticulous attention to detail, this modern villa offers sweeping panoramic views of the city skyline and the Pacific Ocean.

The open-concept floor plan seamlessly integrates indoor and outdoor living. Floor-to-ceiling automated glass doors slide away to reveal a spectacular zero-edge infinity pool, a sunken fire pit, and expansive entertainment decks. The chef's kitchen features custom Italian cabinetry, state-of-the-art Miele appliances, and a massive marble island.

The primary suite is a private sanctuary, boasting a private terrace, a spa-like bathroom with a freestanding soaking tub, and dual walk-in closets. Additional amenities include a fully equipped home theater, a climate-controlled wine cellar, and a cutting-edge smart home automation system.
